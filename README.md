# HR Strategic Audit: ZLK Securities

## Project Overview
An audit of HR practices at ZLK Securities, focusing on how the firm manages and hires its staff. The goal was to find out why the company was struggling to keep its best employees.

## Key Actions
* **Evaluation:** Studied the firm’s current recruitment and performance review processes.
* **Problem Solving:** Identified that outdated performance review systems were causing low team motivation and high turnover.
* **Recommendations:** Proposed a new, fairer policy framework to support better career development and team morale.

## Business Impact
Designed a clear plan for the company to improve its work culture, which helps in retaining talented employees and attracting new ones.

## Tools Used
* **Qualitative Analysis:** Applied HR management frameworks to evaluate organizational effectiveness.
* **Policy Development:** Created evidence-based recommendations for better team management.
